About

Glass & Garnish was created as an assignment for my Computer Science course. As a lover of cocktails and web design, I saw this
as the perfect opportunity to build a website dedicated to showcasing delicious drinks and inspiring others to get creative in the kitchen.
I hope you enjoy exploring and discovering new cocktail recipes as much as I enjoyed building this site!
